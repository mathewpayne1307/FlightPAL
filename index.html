About FlightPAL

FlightPAL is an automated pilot logbook designed to make flight logging fast, accurate, and effortless. This page provides support and information for users of the FlightPAL iOS app.

Frequently Asked Questions

How are flights logged automatically?

FlightPAL uses user-initiated start and stop actions to record block times, airfields, and other flight details automatically.

Can I edit my flight entries?

Yes. All logged flights can be reviewed and edited within the app if any information needs adjusting.

Where does weather data come from?

METAR weather data is provided by the NOAA Aviation Weather Center and is displayed for informational purposes.
